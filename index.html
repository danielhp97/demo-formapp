Frequently Asked Questions
What does my Mental Ratio score mean?
Your Mental Ratio score (0–100) is a weighted average of your six mental performance attributes. It reflects your mental readiness for peak performance, not a fixed measure of ability.
Who sees my assessment results?
Your assessment history is visible to your assigned specialist. Outsider Evaluation requests are reviewed by anonymous scouts — neither party sees the other's identity or scores.
How do I unlock attribute insights?
Completing tasks and submitting assessments earn season points. As you accumulate points you progress through levels (Explorer → Analyst → Specialist → Elite), unlocking written strengths and development notes for each attribute.
What is an Outsider Evaluation?
A premium service where one or more scouts independently evaluate your mental profile based on your assessment history, without seeing your self-scores. €50 per request, delivered in 3–5 working days.
Can I delete my account and data?
Yes. You own your data under GDPR. Request full data deletion via Settings → Data & Privacy.
